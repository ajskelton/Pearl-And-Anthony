Pearl & Anthony

Here's the highlights of our story that led us up to this day.

Dating in Santa Cruz

After many years of friendship the timing was right and Pearl & Anthony began dating April 30th of 2009.

Sacramento Bound

A job offer in Sacramento led to the decision to take their relationship to the next step and move in together.

365 Days

To fuel their creativity they began a portrait project called 365 Days. See the project in its entirety here.

New Year's Eve in NYC

The idea of spending New Year's in Times Square led to a cross country trip to visit close friends and family.

Viva Las Vegas

A regular vacation destination for Pearl & Anthony, they celebrated their two year anniversary in Las Vegas.

Our First Home

Another step in the relationship was marked when they bought their first home in November of 2011.

Annie the Kitten

Annie, Anthony's first pet, joined the family to mark the couple's third anniversary. The Dude is still adjusting.

Green Thumbs

Transforming the backyard took time and effort but building a successful garden was a fun accomplishment.

She Said Yes!

On April 30th 2013 Anthony took Pearl to the beach in Santa Cruz for a picnic and asked her to be his wife.

The Bride

Growing up in Arizona Pearl always knew she would return to the California coast for college. While attending UCSC for a degree in Film Photography she decided to make Santa Cruz her home after graduation. She became part of a great group of friends that held weekly game nights hosted by Anthony. Their friendship grew over the years until the timing was right for the beginning of a wonderful relationship.

The Groom

Anthony grew up in Santa Cruz and returned after finishing college in San Francisco. While persuing a career in television he discovered a passion for photography, which is something he soon learned he shared with Pearl. Their great connection during game nights led to photo walks and the chemistry grew. With a little push from Pearl's best friend Barbara they began their journey together.
